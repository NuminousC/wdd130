Dry Oar Rafting

Welcome to Dry Oar Rafting – where adventure meets safety and unforgettable memories are made on the river. Founded in 2012 by a group of passionate river guides, Dry Oar Rafting was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves the chance to experience the thrill and beauty of white water rafting in a safe, fun, and respectful environment.

History

Dry Oar Rafting was founded in 2012 by four friends who shared an unstoppable passion for rivers and adventure. What began as weekend rafting trips among buddies quickly turned into something much bigger. It all started on the roaring rapids of the Salmon River. After guiding for other companies for several years, Jake Thompson, Sarah Mitchell, and the Rivera brothers (Miguel and Luis) dreamed of creating a rafting company that put people and the river first. They believed rafting should be about more than just adrenaline — it should be about safety, respect for nature, friendship, and creating memories that last a lifetime. With just two rafts and an old pickup truck, they officially launched Dry Oar Rafting in the summer of 2012. Their first season was small — only 14 trips — but their reputation for fun, professional, and safe experiences spread quickly through word of mouth.
